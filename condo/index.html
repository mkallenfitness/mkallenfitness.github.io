Description

Welcome to your beachfront retreat in New Smyrna Beach! This spacious condo offers stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean and is located directly on the no-drive section of the beach, ensuring a peaceful and safe environment.

Location and Local Attractions

  • Local Dining: Explore a variety of dining options, from beachfront cafes to seafood restaurants offering fresh catch of the day.
  • Art and Culture: Visit local art galleries and boutiques, or attend cultural events and festivals that celebrate the community's rich heritage.
  • Outdoor Activities: Experience the thrill of surfing, paddleboarding, or simply lounging under the sun on our no-drive beach. Nearby parks and nature reserves offer hiking trails and wildlife viewing opportunities.

Condo Features and Amenities

  • Swimming Pool: Cool off in the sparkling pool or lounge on the sun deck overlooking the ocean.
  • Balcony: Relax with panoramic views from the private balcony, perfect for morning coffee or sunset cocktails.
  • Full Kitchen: Prepare meals with ease in the fully equipped kitchen, complete with modern appliances and ample counter space.
  • Entertainment: Stay connected with complimentary WiFi and enjoy entertainment on two TVs available in the condo.
  • Bedrooms: Retreat to the spacious primary bedroom featuring a king-sized bed and en suite bathroom with a walk-in shower. The second bedroom is ideal for families with a bunk bed (twin over full) and an additional twin trundle mattress.
  • Beach Gear: Beach chairs, sand toys, boogie boards, and a beach tent are provided for your convenience.
  • Recreational Facilities: Enjoy onsite amenities including shuffleboard courts, tennis courts, and basketball courts.
  • Family-Friendly: A playroom is stocked with toys and board games, ensuring fun activities for children and families.
